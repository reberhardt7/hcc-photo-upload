We will be looking back over the last 20 years at God's great faithfulness to us as a church family, as well as seeking His guidance together for the next chapter. One way we would like to celebrate is to put together some multimedia with photos from our history. If you have any old photos from your time at Harvest, we would appreciate if you could kindly upload them for us!
